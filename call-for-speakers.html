Call for Speakers

Sun 07 April 2013

The call for speakers for PyData Boston (July 27-28th) is now open. If you are interested in presenting a talk on how you use Python for data management, analytics, or visualization or leading a tutorial session, please email a short description of your idea, including whether it is a beginner or advanced presentation, to boston_proposals@pydata.org. The initial call-for-speakers for PyData Boston will be open through May 31st.
